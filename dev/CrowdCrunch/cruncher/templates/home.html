What is CrowdCrunch?

A Virtual Assistant

CrowdCrunch is a virtual assistant built upon the most powerful natural language processor in existence - the human mind. Simply text your wish to the CrowdCrunch number (+1-703-997-8527), and we’ll pair you with one of our virtual assistants. Your wish could be any number of things -- make reservations at a restaurant, cancel a magazine subscription, even make a purchase -- leaving you to focus on the more important parts of your day. Just make sure you’ve budgeted enough credits for the job, and we’ll get it taken care of. You can even communicate with your assistant by addressing them by name:

Nick, …

When the job is complete, either accept or reject it and give us a rating of the user. We use your feedback to ensure that our highest quality assistants remain employed.

A Way to Make Money from your Phone

Simply sign up and text “crunchtime” to begin accepting jobs. When you get paired with a job, and if you accept, simply follow the instructions of the owner, and upon acceptance of your work, receive a credit, which you will later be able to redeem for bitcoin. The higher the quality your work, the higher the quality the job you receive in the future. You can ask clarifying questions by addressing them by name:

Jane, …

A Proof of Concept

CrowdCrunch was built in just under 24 hours, so there are definitely some aspects that can be improved. We currently use a strict parsing system for some commands (declaring budget and such), but we intend to capitalize on the human ability to parse language for everything in the future.
